"An ode to the women who shaped me"
— Sid Hall, Founder

My mother never just made dinner—she created memories around our table. Every Friday night, the smell of her challah would fill our house, and somehow, no matter how many people showed up, there was always enough food for everyone.

She learned from her mother, who learned from hers. Three generations of women who understood that food isn't just sustenance—it's love made tangible. It's how we show people they matter.

I'm opening Jewish Mother's Deli in Historic Williamsburg to honor that legacy. Every recipe comes from my family's kitchen. Every meal will be prepared with the same care my mother and grandmother showed me.

This isn't just a business—it's my way of sharing the love they gave me with this community.
